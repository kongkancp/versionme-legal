# Health & Fitness Disclaimer

VersionMe is a goal tracking application, not a medical service.

PLEASE READ BEFORE USING HEALTH OR FITNESS GOALS:

• The exercise guidance and wellness content in VersionMe is for
  informational and motivational purposes only.

• VersionMe is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice,
  diagnosis, or treatment.

• Always consult your physician or a qualified healthcare provider
  before beginning any new exercise program, especially if you have
  a pre-existing medical condition, injury, or health concern.

• Stop any exercise activity immediately if you experience:
  - Chest pain or pressure
  - Severe shortness of breath
  - Dizziness or fainting
  - Joint pain (not muscle soreness)
  - Any unusual discomfort

• Exercise form guidance provided in missions is general in nature.
  Individual needs may vary. Consider working with a certified
  fitness professional for personalized guidance.

• Mental wellness goal content is for general self-improvement
  purposes only and is not a substitute for professional mental
  health treatment.
